Sophie's Cafe



About us

Sophie's cafe is a self made and family run coffee shop that was started up in November 2019 located in Milton Keynes. It was created by Sophie Burrell who is a young and talented barista and an excellent baker who has been working around the clock trying to create and improve recipes and coffee blends. Here at Sophie's cafe we strive to provide the best tasting coffee and cakes possible while you relax, meetup with friends or tackle important work. To make our coffees we only use responsibly sourced rich columbian coffee beans to provide maximum taste while also supporting the right people. The cakes and pastries available inside are all freshly baked that same morning to ensure the quality of the food is as perfect as it can be.




Don't forget your friends!

At sophie's cafe its not just you who can enjoy our variety of scrumptious cakes and tasty drinks, you can also bring your dogs! That's right, we are a dog friendly cafe so you don't have to leave your best friends at home. All dogs will be given a bowl of water and a handful of treats free of charge which cater to any dietary requirements your dog may have such as wheat free treats.
